About Us

At Universal EFX Studios, we bring imagination to life through world-class visual effects and post-production artistry.

Founded by passionate creators and industry professionals, we specialize in delivering high-quality VFX, Rotoscoping, Paint/Cleanup, Compositing, and Matchmove services for feature films, television, OTT platforms, and commercials.

Over the years, our team has contributed to a wide range of Indian and international projects combining technical precision with creative storytelling. Whether it’s a complex wire removal, an intricate roto sequence, or seamless CG integration, we pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of quality, efficiency, and confidentiality.

We believe that great visuals come from great partnerships. By working closely with filmmakers, studios, and production houses, we ensure every frame tells a story beautifully and powerfully.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized creative hub that consistently delivers exceptional visual effects and inspires the next generation of digital artists.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, scalable, and creative VFX solutions that transform imagination into cinematic reality.
